# 💼 My Favorite Project: Alfa-Business

## 🏦 Banking for Business in Your Pocket

**Alfa-Business** is a mobile banking app that gives entrepreneurs full control over their company's finances right from their smartphone. It's more than just an app — it's an ecosystem for efficient business management.

---

## ⚡ Key Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| 💸 **Payments** | Process RUB and foreign currency payments using any details, sign with a single code, and send confirmations in PDF format |
| 🏢 **Multi-Company Management** | Control all legal entities within your group from a single profile |
| 👥 **Payroll Projects** | Pay employee salaries and manage corporate cards seamlessly |
| 🌍 **Currency Control** | Handle foreign trade contracts and documents for currency compliance |
| 💬 **Support Chat** | 24/7 assistance for any business-related questions |

## 🚀 Latest Innovations

### 🤖 AI Assistant *(September 2025)*

A neural network that:
